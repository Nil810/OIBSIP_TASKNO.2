Remembering Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

My role model Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, also known as the "People's President" and the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. His contributions to space research and education have left an indelible mark on the nation.

Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Dr. Kalam played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts.

Despite his numerous accomplishments, he remained humble and dedicated to inspiring the youth of the nation.

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, but his legacy continues to motivate millions around the world.

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts, and thoughts result in action.” - Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam
